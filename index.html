FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Is it hard to set up?
No. The installer walks you through it. Use the built-in model to start instantly, or paste an API key in under a minute — no technical knowledge required.
Do I need a powerful computer?
For a local model, a decent GPU helps. No GPU? Use a cheap or free API key instead — Oriven works either way. You can start with a free API key and switch to local later.
Is my data really private?
Yes. There's no account and no telemetry on your conversations. With a local model, nothing you say ever touches the internet. Your character files live on your computer — only you can access them.
What can I do with it?
One-on-one chat with persistent characters, multi-character group scenes, long-form collaborative stories, voice conversations (text-to-speech and speech-to-text), and AI-generated character images.
Which languages does it support?
English, Turkish, Spanish, Portuguese, German, French, Russian, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese — with natural, native-feeling output, not machine translation.
One-time price — really no subscription?
Really. Buy Pro once and it's yours. Future minor updates are included free. A major version upgrade in the future may be a discounted paid upgrade for existing owners — but that's not a monthly fee, and you keep your current version forever.
Can I get a refund?
Yes — 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If it doesn't work on your PC, you get your money back.
Is the free version crippled?
No — it's a real, fully functional private chat app you can use forever. Pro adds the full world: voice, images, group chat, story mode, unlimited characters, and all 10 languages.
